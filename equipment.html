The right stabilizer makes the difference between a beautiful design and a puckered mess. Here are the ones I use every day.

✂️
Most Used

Tear-Away Stabilizer

For stable woven fabrics like cotton, canvas, denim, and linen. Sulky Tear-Easy is my daily driver — it tears cleanly without leaving residue or distorting the design.

Best for cotton & linen
Tears away cleanly

Cut-Away Stabilizer

Essential for stretchy fabrics — t-shirts, polo shirts, jersey knits. Stays permanently in the design to prevent distortion over time. Pellon 70 is a reliable budget option.

For Towels

Used on top of terry cloth, fleece, and velvet to prevent stitches from sinking into the pile. Dissolves completely when dampened with water. Sulky Solvy is the gold standard.
