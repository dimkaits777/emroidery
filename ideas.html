What Can You Make?

Turn beautiful embroidery designs into treasured handmade gifts. Here are 8 project ideas to inspire you — with links to the perfect designs for each.

🍽️

Embroidered Kitchen Towels

Terry cloth and linen kitchen towels are a blank canvas for beautiful embroidery. A floral design or a sweet quote transforms an everyday item into a luxurious gift. Tip: use a tear-away stabilizer for best results on towels.

Shop Floral Designs →
👜

Personalized Tote Bags

Natural cotton tote bags with an embroidered design are practical, eco-friendly, and utterly charming. Add her name or a meaningful quote for an extra personal touch. Great for grocery runs or the beach!

Shop Quote Designs →
🧥

Denim Jackets with Floral Embroidery

Upcycle a plain denim jacket into a wearable masterpiece. Floral designs on the back or collar are incredibly trendy. Use a cutaway stabilizer and a sharp needle for best results on denim.

Shop Floral Designs →
🛋️

Throw Pillows with Quotes

An embroidered pillow with a heartfelt quote is a gift that decorates her home and warms her heart. Choose a design that matches her décor — florals for a classic look, modern minimalist for contemporary spaces.

Shop Quote Designs →
👶

Baby Onesies & Blankets

Celebrate new mamas with sweet embroidered onesies, burp cloths, or swaddle blankets. These make incredibly touching gifts for baby showers. Use a water-soluble stabilizer on soft knit fabrics.

Shop New Mom Designs →
🖼️

Framed Hoop Art

Leave your embroidery right in the wooden hoop and hang it as wall art. It's quick to frame, looks incredibly handmade-chic, and costs almost nothing. Add a ribbon on top for a gift-ready finish.

Shop Modern Designs →
🔑

Keychains & Luggage Tags

Small felt or vinyl keychains with tiny embroidered designs are perfect for last-minute gifts or party favors. Back them with felt, add a grommet, and attach a keyring — done in under an hour!

Browse All Designs →
👩‍🍳

Aprons for Mom

A personalised apron is one of the most-used and most-loved gifts you can give a cooking mom. Embroider her name, a sweet quote, or a whimsical food-related design on the bib front. She'll think of you every time she cooks!
