About Stitch & Bloom

A labour of love, built for makers who want to create beautiful, heartfelt gifts for the moms in their lives.

Our Story

Stitch & Bloom was born from a simple belief: the most meaningful gifts are the ones made by hand. We're a small team of embroidery enthusiasts who curate the best machine embroidery designs for Mother's Day — so you can spend less time searching and more time stitching.

Every design in our collection has been selected for its quality, versatility, and heartfelt potential. Whether you're embroidering a kitchen towel for your own mom, a tote bag for a grandmother, or a tiny onesie for a brand-new mama — we have the perfect design for you.

All designs are sold through our trusted partner, Creative Fabrica. When you purchase through our links, you support this site at no extra cost to you.
